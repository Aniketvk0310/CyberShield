Key Threat Categories

Cyber threats evolve constantly. Focus on the big categories to build resilience in every layer of defense.

  • Malware and ransomware
  • Social engineering and phishing
  • Network intrusion and DDoS
  • Insider threats and misconfigurations

Visual cues help students identify patterns in attack techniques.

Malware

Malicious software that steals data, disrupts systems, or encrypts files for ransom.

Phishing

Fake messages that trick users into revealing passwords or clicking unsafe links.

DDoS

Flooding a target with traffic to make services unavailable.
